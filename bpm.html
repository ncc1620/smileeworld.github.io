Description
Blackbird Pie Media is the post-production, archival, and creative direction studio founded by Lee Richard Grimwade (SmiLee). Operating as a core subdivision of YOU ARE HERE Creative Studios, BPM specializes in documentary editing, audio mastering, archival restoration, creative metadata structuring, and transmedia storytelling. The studio manages finishing workflows, creative supervision, and the preservation of historical media across the SmiLeeWorld ecosystem.
